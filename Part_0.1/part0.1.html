K L University

The Koneru Lakshmaiah Charities was established as a trust in the year 1980 with its official address at Museum road, Vijayawada and started KL College of Engineering in the Academic year 1980-81.K L University is situated in a spacious 50-acre campus on the banks of Buckingham Canal of river Krishna, eight kilometers from Vijayawada city.
